Rules & Regulations

All participants are required to read and abide by the official competition rules set by SIMCC and The Brainiac Factory Cambodia.

General Rules
Student Conduct & Contest Rules
📋

Eligibility

Students must compete in the grade division corresponding to their current school year. Registering in a lower grade is prohibited.

⏱️

Punctuality

Students must arrive at least 15 minutes before the start time. Latecomers may not receive additional time.

🚫

Prohibited Items

Mobile phones, calculators unless specified, smart watches, and communication devices are prohibited.

🤝

Academic Integrity

Cheating, communication, plagiarism, or unauthorized materials will result in disqualification.

Online Contest Rules
Guidelines for Online Competitions

Technical Requirements

  • A stable internet connection and suitable device are required.
  • Login credentials are provided after registration.
  • Technical difficulties must be reported immediately.

Supervision

  • Online contests may be supervised by schools or The Brainiac Factory.
  • Parents must not assist students.
  • Screen recording and screenshots are forbidden.
Disqualification Policy
Grounds for Disqualification
ViolationConsequence
Unauthorized deviceImmediate disqualification, no refund
Communication with another studentImmediate disqualification
Incorrect grade divisionResults voided
Sharing contest content publiclyDisqualification and possible future ban
ImpersonationDisqualification of both parties

Appeals

Appeals must be submitted in writing within 7 days of the result announcement. SIMCC's final decision is binding.
