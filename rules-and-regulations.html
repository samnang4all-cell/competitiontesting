Rules & Regulations

All participants are required to read and abide by the official competition rules set by SIMCC and The Brainiac Factory Cambodia.

General Rules
Student Conduct & Contest Rules
📋

Eligibility

Students must compete in the grade division corresponding to their current school year at the time of registration. Over-grading (registering in a lower grade) is strictly prohibited.

⏱️

Punctuality

Students must arrive at the contest venue at least 15 minutes before the start time. Latecomers may not receive additional time.

🚫

Prohibited Items

Mobile phones, calculators (unless specified), smart watches, and any electronic communication devices are strictly prohibited during the contest.

🤝

Academic Integrity

All work must be the student's own. Cheating, plagiarism, communication with other participants, or use of unauthorized materials will result in immediate disqualification.

Online Contest Rules
Guidelines for Online Competitions

Technical Requirements

  • A stable internet connection is required throughout the contest
  • Students must use a device (laptop, desktop, or tablet) with a working camera and browser
  • Login credentials will be provided after registration confirmation
  • Technical difficulties must be reported to the proctor immediately — no extra time is granted for connectivity issues

Supervision

  • Online contests may be conducted under school supervision or at The Brainiac Factory's premises
  • Parents supervising home-based online contests must not assist the student in any way
  • Screen recording or screenshot during the contest is strictly forbidden
Disqualification Policy
Grounds for Disqualification
Violation Consequence
Use of mobile phone or unauthorized device during contest Immediate disqualification, no refund
Communication with another student during contest Immediate disqualification
Registering in an incorrect grade division Results voided
Sharing contest questions or content publicly Disqualification and possible ban from future contests
Impersonation (another student sitting the contest) Disqualification of both parties

Appeals

Any appeal regarding scores or disqualification must be submitted in writing to The Brainiac Factory within 7 days of the result announcement. SIMCC's final decision is binding.
