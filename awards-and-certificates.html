Awards & Certificates

SIMCC recognizes outstanding student achievement through internationally respected medals, certificates, trophies, and honour rolls.

Award Categories
Medal & Certificate Tiers
🥇

Gold Medal

Awarded to top-scoring students who achieve the highest marks in their grade division.

🥈

Silver Medal

Awarded to students who demonstrate excellence in the upper tier of their grade division.

🥉

Bronze Medal

Awarded to students who show strong performance above the general pass standard.

🏆

Trophy

Special trophies are presented to overall highest scorers or global finals winners.

📜

Merit Certificate

Awarded for commendable effort and achievement above the passing threshold.

🌟

Perfect Scorer

Special recognition for students who achieve a perfect score.

Certificate Validity

SIMCC certificates provide a permanent record of academic achievement. Students should keep their digital and printed certificates safe and confirm competition-specific verification procedures with the organizer.
